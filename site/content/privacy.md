## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

### What we collect

**meshsat.net website**: We use [Umami](https://umami.is/) for privacy-friendly analytics. Umami does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and is self-hosted on our infrastructure. No data is shared with third parties.

**MeshSat Bridge**: The Bridge runs entirely on your device. No telemetry is sent. No data leaves your network unless you explicitly configure a satellite, cellular, or IP transport to relay messages.

**MeshSat Hub**: Hub accounts require authentication. We store the minimum data needed to operate the service: email, device registry, and message history (within your configured retention period). We do not sell or share user data.
